About Me
My name is Alexandra Tran (Alex). Welcome to my little piece of the Web. I am a recent college graduate based in the San Francisco Bay Area. By day, I enjoy exploring, analyzing, and clarifying technical concepts, understanding how people learn, and building communities. I am a longtime contributor at DAGlabs, a cryptocurrency project coming out of the Israeli academic scene. I have a background in computer science, blockchain technology, and philosophy. I am an advocate for radical decentralization and voluntary societies, and am excited about projects that empower individuals, for today and the long term.
